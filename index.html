2. Optional SMS import (Android)

Qoondeeye offers an optional SMS auto-import feature to help record transaction alerts sent by supported local mobile money services, banking institutions, and telecommunication providers.

Permissions

If you enable SMS import, the app requests the Android RECEIVE_SMS permission so it can detect incoming financial notifications. We do not request access to read your existing SMS inbox or history, and we do not monitor, read, or store personal conversations.

What messages are processed

The app processes only recognized, automated financial transaction receipts and mobile wallet notifications. All other personal messages, text verification codes, and unrelated SMS communications are completely ignored. Qoondeeye does not read or scan your historical SMS archive.

How SMS data is used

transaction details—such as transaction amounts, transaction type (income, expense, transfer, top-up, or other supported transaction types), timestamps, balances, reference IDs, and masked sender/recipient identifiers where available.

Your control

SMS import is entirely voluntary and remains disabled by default. You can revoke this permission or toggle the feature off within the application settings at any time.

4. Storage & sync

Your records may be stored on your device and synced with backend services (such as Supabase) for backup and multi-device access. Optional receipt images and profile photos may be stored in associated cloud file storage for the same purpose. We do not upload your full SMS inbox, and queued SMS imports store parsed transaction fields only, not your full SMS message body.
